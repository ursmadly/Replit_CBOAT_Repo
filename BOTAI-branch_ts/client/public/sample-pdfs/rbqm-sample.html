RBQM Framework Documentation

Clinical Business Orchestration and AI Technology (cBOAT)

Version 1.0 - April 2025

Overview

Harnessing business Orchestration technology with AI to optimize the management and oversight of clinical trials involves the integration of various profiles and dimensions for comprehensive oversight.

Integrated Profiles

Risk Profile, Quality Profile, Compliance Profile, Safety Profile, Vendor Profile, Financial Profile, Resources Profile

Key Dimensions

Portfolio, Program, Trial, Country, Site, Patient, Supply Chain, Vendor (Service Provider), Budget Planning and Utilization, User Allocations and Assignments

Key Drivers

Integrated Data Review and automated business orchestration with HITL (Human in the loop) to analyse leading and lagging indicators, quality and compliance risks, protocol deviation, risks.

Data Sources

EDC, CTMS, iRT, LIMS, Supply Chain, Data Lake (Patient and Operational)

Benefits of RBQM

  • Improves Patient Safety – Ensures that issues like adverse events or protocol deviations are identified early.
  • Enhances Data Quality – Focuses on critical data points rather than verifying all data, improving efficiency.
  • Reduces Costs – Cuts unnecessary site visits and manual data verification, saving time and money.
  • Meets Regulatory Expectations – Aligns with FDA and ICH E6 (R2) guidelines, which emphasize risk-based approaches.
  • Optimizes Resources – Allows teams to focus on high-risk sites, critical endpoints, and key safety measures.

Risk Detection & Task Creation

  • Parse data from sources: EDC, CTMS, iRT, LIMS, Supply Chain, Data Lake (Patient and Operational)
  • Identify risks automatically using AI/ML models (protocol deviations, data inconsistencies)
  • Generate tasks with override options and manual creation capability
  • Categorize tasks into Critical, High, Medium, Low priority
  • Assign SLAs and calculate due dates

Task Assignment & Workflow Management

  • Persona-Based Assignment of tasks routed to relevant study team members
  • Task Dependencies & Escalations if tasks remain unresolved beyond SLA
  • Task routing for review and approval before further investigation
  • Delegation of tasks for a specified period of time

Alerts & Notifications

  • Email & In-App Notifications for new signal detection, tasks, and approvals
  • SLA Reminders based on urgency and due dates
  • User-configurable Custom Alerts and notifications

Profile Creation

  • Risk, Quality, Compliance, Safety, Vendor, Financial, and Resources profiles
  • Based on Signal detection and task closure assessments
  • Performance ratings for vendors, labs, CRAs and sites
